Catch the falling stars


How to play?

use left arrow or key A to move the basket to the left
use right arrow or key D to move the basket to the right


In this game, your goal is to catch the falling stars to earn points.
You have 3 lives
A star falling on earth means a life lost
whereas catching a golden star earns you a new life.
